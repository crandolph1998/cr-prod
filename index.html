What I Do

I offer a full suite of content services designed to drive growth at every stage of the funnel.

Content Writing

I create content that not only catches attention but builds immediate trust. This positions your brand as an authority in your market, ranking for valuable keywords and delivering a consistent stream of organic traffic.

Conversion & Email Copy

Familiar with Dan Kennedy-esque direct response, I write sales pages and email copy that converts. For B2B or B2C, I develop strategies to retain and monetize traffic, reducing your reliance on paid advertising platforms.

Technical Writing

Beyond standard blog posts, I write in-depth technical content on complex topics like virtualization, B2B, web scraping, and SaaS. I make intricate concepts clear, engaging, and valuable to a technical audience.

SEO Strategy

Content alone doesn't get you ranked. My process begins with ensuring your site is technically optimized, followed by building a strong backlink portfolio that looks natural to Google. I handle the full strategy for you.

Video Copy & Scripts

Whether you need a new video sales letter (VSL) that converts or you're trying to expand your organic YouTube traffic, I can write compelling scripts that capture attention and drive action.

Content Strategy

I go beyond just a list of keywords. I develop comprehensive content roadmaps based on competitor analysis, market positioning, and your business goals to build lasting topical authority and a predictable pipeline of organic traffic.
